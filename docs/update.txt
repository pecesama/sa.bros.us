=== Updating v1.7 to v1.75 ===

	1. Create a backup of your /include/config.php and /include/config.ini files.
	2. Replace all files from v1.7 with those from the new 1.75 EXCEPT for the files /include/config.php and /include/config.ini
		2.1. If you do replace it by error, use the backup you create during step one.
	3. Execute the exportar-ini.php file.
	4. Execute the archivo actualizar.php file, a blank page will appear, just goto index.php after.


=== Updating from any previous version to v1.7 ===

	1. Update to sabros.us 1.7 then follow the above instructions.
